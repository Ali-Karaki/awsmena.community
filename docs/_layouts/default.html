Welcome to MENA Game Tech Day

AWS Game Tech Day MENA is an online event designed to bring the MENA game development ecosystem together to share ideas, exchange information, and discuss how cloud technologies are helping game studios of all sizes. If you are in the process of conceiving your game as an idea, or are interested in understanding player trends, or want to integrate a particular technology into your game, AWS Game Tech Day will help you acquire the knowledge and tools needed to test and validate your thinking and approach, and then take your game to the next level.
